To Build A Fire

To Build A Fire is a survival game coded on the platform Khan Academy. The premise of the game is that you are stuck in the middle of nowhere with nothing, and you need to try to keep your health above zero (survival). You can use WASD to move, and you can click to harvest items.

Further Instructions

There are things that are out to kill you. Wolves are out to kill you by taking your health directly, while rabbits are out to kill you by stealing the food that you need. Unfortunately, as of this stage, you cannot kill these creatures, but what you can do is sprint (SHIFT) to escape animals and to collect berries (which increase your food). Food is important! If you don't have food, your health begins decreasing! Make sure your orange food bar stays above zero by collecting berries.

There are other things you can harvest, like wood and stone. You need a wooden pickaxe to harvest stone. Recipes show up as colored squares in the upper-left corner. If you hover over them, they reveal the name of their recipe. The ones you should care about are fire which increases your health for a short period of time, trail mix, made of berries that increase your food, and pickaxe, which is used to harvest the stone to build a fire. Good luck!
